# Internal Audit Checklist
CompanyX ISO/IEC 27001:2022  
Last Updated: YYYY-MM-DD

## Purpose
Provide a structured checklist for conducting annual ISO 27001 internal audits covering Clauses 4–10 and Annex A controls.

---

## Clause-by-Clause Checklist

### Clause 4 – Context of Organization
- [ ] Internal issues identified?
- [ ] External issues documented?
- [ ] Interested parties & requirements listed?
- [ ] ISMS scope documented?

### Clause 5 – Leadership
- [ ] Leadership demonstrates commitment?
- [ ] Roles and responsibilities defined?
- [ ] Information security policy approved?

### Clause 6 – Planning
- [ ] Risk methodology documented?
- [ ] Information security objectives defined?
- [ ] Risk register maintained & updated?
- [ ] Treatment plans documented?

### Clause 7 – Support
- [ ] Competence requirements documented?
- [ ] Training performed?
- [ ] Awareness documented?
- [ ] ISMS documentation controlled?

### Clause 8 – Operation
- [ ] Operational controls implemented?
- [ ] Change management functioning?
- [ ] Incident management documented?
- [ ] Backups validated?

### Clause 9 – Performance Evaluation
- [ ] Internal audits conducted?
- [ ] Management review performed?
- [ ] KPIs monitored?

### Clause 10 – Improvement
- [ ] Corrective actions tracked?
- [ ] Nonconformities documented?
- [ ] ISMS improvements implemented?

---

## Annex A Control Checklist (Consolidated Summary)

### A.5 Organizational Controls
- [ ] Access control processes effective?
- [ ] Threat intelligence used?
- [ ] Risk assessments current?
- [ ] Vendor security validated?
- [ ] Incident response tested?

### A.7 Physical Controls
- [ ] Remote work requirements followed?
- [ ] AWS & vendor DC controls reviewed?

### A.8 Technological Controls
- [ ] IAM controls enforced?  
- [ ] Encryption enabled where required?  
- [ ] Logging complete & monitored?  
- [ ] Vulnerabilities remediated on time?  
- [ ] Secure development lifecycle functioning?  
- [ ] Malware protection and EDR active?  
- [ ] Backups tested?  
- [ ] Cloud security configured properly?
