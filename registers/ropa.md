# CompanyX Record of Processing Activities (RoPA)
ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Annex A: A.5.34 · GDPR Article 30
Last Updated: 2026-07-02

## Purpose
Maintain the Record of Processing Activities (RoPA) required by IT Security21 (Data Protection & Privacy Policy) Section 3 and GDPR Article 30: a single inventory of every processing activity CompanyX carries out involving personal data, sufficient to demonstrate accountability to a regulator on request.

This register was previously referenced by IT Security21 as being "maintained in the Compliance Register" (`legal-regulatory-register.md`), but that register tracks obligations at the level of a legal framework (e.g. "GDPR — Compliant"), not individual processing activities with the fields Article 30 requires. This file is the actual RoPA; `legal-regulatory-register.md` continues to track the higher-level obligation.

## How to Use This Register
This is a blank template. A real deployment of this ISMS must populate one row per processing activity — for example, "customer account data," "employee payroll data," "support ticket data," "AI tool usage logs containing personal data" — not one row per data subject or per system.

## Review
This register must be reviewed at least annually and updated immediately when a new processing activity commences, an existing one materially changes, or a new cross-border data transfer mechanism is put in place, per IT Security21 Section 3.
