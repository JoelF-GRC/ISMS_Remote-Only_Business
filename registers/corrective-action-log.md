# CompanyX Corrective Action / Nonconformity Log
ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clause 10.1–10.2
Last Updated: YYYY-MM-DD

## Purpose
Track nonconformities, their root causes, corrective actions, and effectiveness reviews, satisfying Clause 10.1 (Continual improvement) and Clause 10.2 (Nonconformity and corrective action).

## Sources of Nonconformities
- Internal or external audit findings
- Security incidents and near misses
- Risk assessment results
- Monitoring/KPI deviations
- Stakeholder feedback or complaints

## Process
1. Nonconformity identified and logged below.
2. Immediate containment action taken (if applicable).
3. Root cause analysis performed.
4. Corrective action defined, owned, and dated.
5. Action implemented and verified.
6. Effectiveness reviewed (typically at the next management review) before closure.

## Status Definitions
- **Open** – Nonconformity identified, action not yet started.
- **In Progress** – Corrective action being implemented.
- **Implemented** – Action complete, pending effectiveness review.
- **Closed** – Action verified effective; nonconformity resolved.

## Reporting
Open items and their status must be reviewed at each management review (see [management-review.md](../ISMS-policies/management-review.md)).

## Ownership
Security Department (ISMS Manager)
