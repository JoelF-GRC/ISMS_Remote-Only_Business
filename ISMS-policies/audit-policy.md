# Internal Audit Policy

## Purpose
Ensure ISMS effectiveness through planned internal audits.

## Requirements
- Annual internal audit covering all ISO 27001 clauses and controls.
- Independent, qualified auditor (internal or external party).
- Findings documented with corrective actions.
- Follow-up audits required for major nonconformities.

## Reporting
Audit results are presented during management review.

## Ownership
Security Department
