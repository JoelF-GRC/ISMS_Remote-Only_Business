# Continual Improvement Policy

## Purpose
Ensure that CompanyX continuously improves its information security posture.

## Sources of Improvement
- Audit findings
- Incidents and near misses
- Metrics and objectives
- Risk assessment results
- Stakeholder feedback

## Actions
- Corrective actions recorded and tracked
- Root cause analysis for major issues
- Annual review of ISMS performance

## Ownership
Security Department
