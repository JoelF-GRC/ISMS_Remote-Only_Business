# Management Overview Policy

## Leadership Responsibilities
- Demonstrate commitment to the ISMS.
- Allocate resources and budget.
- Approve ISMS scope, objectives, and policies.
- Participate in annual management review.
- Promote continual improvement.

## Management Review
Occurs annually and covers:
- Audit results
- Risk register updates
- Incident trends
- KPIs
- Supplier performance
- Opportunities for improvement

## Decision-Making
Leadership decisions directly impact risk acceptance, policy approval, and resourcing.
