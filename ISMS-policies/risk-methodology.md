# ISMS Risk Assessment Methodology

## Purpose
Define how risks are identified, analyzed, evaluated, and treated.

## Method
1. Identify assets, threats, vulnerabilities.
2. Assign risk likelihood and impact:
   - Low / Medium / High
3. Calculate inherent risk.
4. Define controls and treatments.
5. Assign residual risk.
6. Record in risk-register.md.
7. Assign owners and due dates.
8. Climate-related environmental risks (flood, wildfire, energy instability) must be considered when identifying threats or evaluating the likelihood of disruption scenarios.


## Review
- Performed annually or when significant changes occur.
- Risk acceptance requires Security and Leadership approval.

## Tools
- Jira
- Risk Register
- Threat Intelligence
