# ISMS Scope Statement

## Purpose
Define the scope and boundaries of the CompanyX Information Security Management System (ISMS).

## Scope
The ISMS covers all information assets, processes, people, and technology supporting the operation of CompanyX’s remote-first SaaS platform hosted primarily on:
- AWS (IaaS)
- Entra ID (Identity Provider)
- Jira (work management)
- CrowdStrike (endpoint + identity protection)
- Rapid7 (SIEM)
- Endpoints used by employees and contractors

## In-Scope Areas
- Product engineering
- Cloud infrastructure (AWS)
- Identity and access management (Entra ID)
- DevOps and CI/CD
- Security operations
- Third-party vendor management
- Business continuity
- Incident response
- Risk management processes
- Environmental and climate-related risks affecting continuity or system availability


## Out-of-Scope Areas
- Non-CompanyX owned personal devices  
- Corporate office locations (CompanyX is remote-only)  

## Shared Responsibility (High-Level)
- AWS is responsible for physical infrastructure security.
- CompanyX is responsible for secure configuration, workloads, data, identities, and applications.
