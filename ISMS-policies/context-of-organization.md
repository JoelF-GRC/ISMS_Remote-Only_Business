# Context of the Organization

## Purpose
Define internal and external issues that influence CompanyX’s ability to achieve ISMS objectives.

## Internal Issues
- Rapid product development cycles
- Remote-first workforce
- Dependence on cloud-native services
- Limited physical footprint

## External Issues
- Evolving cybersecurity threat landscape
- Customer security expectations
- Regulatory obligations
- Vendor dependencies (AWS, SaaS platforms)
### Environmental & Climate Considerations
CompanyX recognizes that climate change may cause:
- Regional cloud outages
- Power instability or ISP disruptions affecting remote workers
- Disruptions to critical suppliers
- Transportation/logistics delays impacting hardware
Climate factors are tracked as external issues and reviewed annually.
## Interested Parties & Requirements
| Interested Party | Needs & Expectations |
|------------------|----------------------|
| Customers | Secure SaaS, uptime, encryption, incident response |
| Regulators | Compliance, data protection |
| Employees | Clear security roles, secure tools |
| Vendors | Defined access and requirements |
| Management | Risk visibility, performance metrics |

## Scope Interaction
Context informs the scope, objectives, risk methodology, and policies.

## Maintenance
Reviewed annually or after significant organizational change.
