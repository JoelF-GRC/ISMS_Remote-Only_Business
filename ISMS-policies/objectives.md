# Information Security Objectives

CompanyX maintains measurable information security objectives aligned to business goals.

## Objectives
- Maintain confidentiality, integrity, and availability of SaaS platform.
- Ensure uptime >= 99.9% for production services.
- Complete quarterly access reviews with 100% participation.
- Resolve critical vulnerabilities within 7 days.
- Complete annual internal audits and management reviews.
- Ensure compliance with ISO 27001 and customer commitments.

## Measurement
Metrics are recorded quarterly and reviewed during management review.
