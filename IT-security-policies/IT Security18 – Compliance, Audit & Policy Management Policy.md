# IT Security18 – Compliance, Audit & Policy Management Policy
(Annex A: A.5.31, A.5.32, A.5.35, A.5.36)

## Overview
CompanyX maintains a structured compliance and audit program to meet legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements. This policy defines how policies are created, reviewed, approved, and maintained, and how internal and external audits are conducted.

## Policy

### 1. Compliance Obligations  
(Annex A: A.5.31)
- CompanyX must identify all applicable compliance obligations, including:
  - ISO 27001
  - SOC 2
  - Data protection laws (e.g., GDPR equivalents)
  - Contractual security requirements from customers
- Obligations must be documented and reviewed annually.

### 2. Internal Audits  
(Annex A: A.5.35)
- Internal ISMS audits must occur at least annually.
- Audit scope must include:
  - Policies and procedures
  - Technical controls
  - Risk treatment progress
  - Evidence of operational effectiveness
- Findings must be recorded and tracked to closure.

### 3. External Audits  
(Annex A: A.5.35)
- External auditors may evaluate:
  - Information security controls
  - Cloud configurations
  - Identity and access controls
  - Incident response readiness
- All external audit requests must be coordinated through Security.

### 4. Nonconformities & Corrective Actions  
(Annex A: A.5.36)
- Nonconformities must be documented in the Corrective Action Register.
- Root cause analysis must be performed for significant issues.
- Corrective actions must be completed within defined timelines.

### 5. Policy Lifecycle Management  
(Annex A: A.5.36)
- Policies must:
  - Follow a standard template
  - Be reviewed annually
  - Be tracked in a Document Control Register
- Policy approval requires the CISO or executive delegate.

### 6. Policy Distribution & Awareness  
(Annex A: A.6.3)
- All employees must confirm acceptance of information security policies.
- Policy updates must be communicated via email, training, or internal announcements.

### 7. Evidence Management  
- Evidence supporting compliance must be:
  - Stored securely
  - Version controlled
  - Retained according to data retention policies

## Policy Conflicts
This policy supersedes local or ad hoc audit practices unless formally approved.

## Enforcement
Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action, up to termination.

## Ownership
Security Department (Governance, Risk & Compliance)
